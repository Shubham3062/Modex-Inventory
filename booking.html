Frequently Asked Questions

When You Need An Inventory?

+

You need an inventory when managing properties, ensuring accuracy during check-ins/outs, preventing disputes, and maintaining detailed records.

Why do I need an inventory?

+

No matter how well a tenant looks after the property, it will not be in the same condition at the end of the tenancy as it was at its start. It is extremely important to have a very detailed inventory report of the property at the start of the tenancy, with evidence for both sides.

Can you collect/return property keys?

+

Yes, we will collect the keys from the letting agent or landlord. Once the inventory is complete we will also label all the keys so if you need to cut copies for the new tenants, you won’t have to revisit the property.

How to book an inventory?

+

You don’t have to check for availability, just click here to book your property and we will contact you once we receive your order.

How you receive the report?

+

We will email you a detailed written report with high-quality photographs within 24 hours. If you provide us with the tenants or any other party’s email, they will also receive a copy instantly, so there is nothing else left for you to do.
